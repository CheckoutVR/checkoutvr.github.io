Modern Trail Maps

  • In an age of rapid technological progress, trail maps have stayed stagnant since their inception
  • The good old PDF version does its job but we believe the trail could be greatly improved

Our Platform

  • SlopeScope is a web platform that takes the basic PDF version of a resort's trail map and turns it into a more informative and interactive image
  • This eliminates the need to flip-flop back and forth between terrain status pages and PDF trail maps to find the best open runs

Other Features

  • SlopeScope also provides grooming information and everything else you would find on a terrain status pages
  • We also plan to add more useful information for each run, like average skier density

The trail map can be a crucial first step in finding the perfect run and we hope to provide users with as much information as possible before they make their decision!
