About

In an age of rapidly progressing technology, trail maps have remained stagnant since their inception. Take a look at the website for your favorite ski resort and you will find a PDF version of the trail map. It's undeniable that the good old PDF version does its job, but we believe the trail map could be greatly improved.

SlopeScope is a web platform that takes the basic PDF version of a resort's trail map and turns it into a more informative and interactive image. While before, determining the location of open terrain required flipping back between a resort's Terrain Status page and the pdf trail map, SlopeScope combines this information in real time, allowing the user to quickly visualize what lifts and runs are open.

SlopeScope doesn't simply stop at terrain status information. It allows viewers to see the grooming status of runs and we plan to include even more information for each run, like average skier density.

At SlopeScope we know that the trail map can be a crucial first step in finding the perfect runs and we hope to provide users with as much information as possible before they make their decision!
